Our Club

Over the past eight years, STEM Reach has hosted many fun and exciting hands-on events. From Micro:Bit to Java camps, we have developed curricula to teach a wide array of elementary and middle school kids. Members of STEM Reach will help create curricula and host teaching events. Apart from the experiences, skills, and volunteer hours that STEM Reach members will gain, they will also get the opportunity to make an impact by introducing kids to STEM!

Our Mission & Vision

Through STEM Reach, we aim to teach many underrepresented and underserved individuals about STEM.

Some of the project topics we have covered include coding in Scratch, Python, Arduino, micro:bit, and more! Along with hands-on learning, students develop communication, teamwork, organization, perseverance, and problem-solving skills.
