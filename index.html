Welcome to the STEM Reach Website

It's Never Too Early to Start Learning

Technology is becoming increasingly prominent in our day to day lives. Developing skills, especially at an early age, to succeed in our modernized future is very valuable. STEM Reach aims to help kids from elementary to middle school learn the basics of coding.


Science

Science, on a basic level, is the skill of hypothesizing and experimenting. Our events give kids the opportunity to experiment and discover on their own with guidance from our student teachers!

Technology

Technology is all around us, including our phones, iPads, televisions and laptops. Our events center around teaching the basics of coding, programming robots with micro:bit and much more!

Engineering

Engineering is the idea of designing and building robots and machines. MakerClub events allow kids to explore the design process and use provided materials to assemble their creation.

Math

Mathematics, the study of quantities, can be as complex as it can be simple. The coding taught at our events includes math basics and is reiterated by our student teachers to ensure every child's understanding.
